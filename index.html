What is Smart Irrigation?

Smart Irrigation means different things to different people, but one thing it has in common—saving our most precious natural resource—fresh water. SmartIrrigation.com is an irrigation information website with that goal and more. Our goal is not only saving water, but also saving you time, money and trouble when it comes to landscape irrigation design techniques, products and installation. We do not recommend, advertise or promote irrigation products that we ourselves would not use. Our advice and tips come from decades of irrigation experience with both residential and commercial applications. We hope you enjoy this site and find it valuable.
